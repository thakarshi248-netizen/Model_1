About

How it started

Aurevo began with two people united by a passion for coffee. Clara and Marek connected while traveling in Europe, discovering cafés tucked away in quiet streets, gaining inspiration along the way.

Upon returning home, they turned their dreams into reality, transforming a small kitchen roaster into Aurevo, a labor of love for quality and community.

Today, Aurevo embodies the values of authenticity and connection, where every bean roasted and pastry baked adds to their story—and yours.

We Value Equality

Coffee brings people together — no matter who you are, you belong at Aurevo. Our team shares one mission: to create a space where everyone feels welcome, cared for, and part of the story.

People and culture

At Aurevo, every cup is crafted by a dedicated team, combining the skills of baristas and bakers to create a warm atmosphere where customers feel cherished. Our beverages reflect our commitment to quality and storytelling.

We believe coffee is an experience that unites people.
